Formula 1 Through the Ages

This project is a look through the history of Formula 1, with individual pages styled to and containing relevant information about each of the 8 decades Formula 1 has been around. You can take a peek at the fantastical beginnings of this sport in the 1950s to the new technology and stars inspiring millions of people in the 2020s. Each webpage contains fun facts and information about that decade as a whole, including driver interviews wherever possible, the standout drivers and teams of that decade, as well as some pictures of historical tracks and cars that were used.
