Welcome to the 1950s!

This was the first decade of the formula 1 championship, running in format very different to what it is nowadays. The format back then was as follows: 7+ championship races (it changed through the seasons) throughout the season with points awarded to the top 5 finishers on an 8-6-4-3-2 basis, with an extra point for getting the fastest lap of the race. However, only the best 4 results counted toward the championship.
